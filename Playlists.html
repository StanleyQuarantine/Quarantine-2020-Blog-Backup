I sure do! It depends on what kind of music you're interested in.

Ska

Are you interested in Ska? I love Mustard Plug. They're a Ska band from Colorado, and they are a more traditional Ska band, utilizing the usual upbeat tempos, brass, and it is typical skanking music. I got to see them at the "Back to the Beach" Festival and they played two of my favorite songs, Beer and Mr. Smiley. Mr. Smiley is the song that got me looking more into their music.
