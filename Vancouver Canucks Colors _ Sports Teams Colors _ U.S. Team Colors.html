Vancouver Canucks Colors

NHL team colors

The official Vancouver Canucks team colors are blue, green and white. The color codes: RGB, CYMK for print, Hex for web and the Vancouver Canucks Pantone colors can be seen below.

You can use these color codes for your Canucks fan site, blog, or even painting one of your rooms in the colors of your favorite hockey team.
